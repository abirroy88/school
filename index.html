Welcome to HIRS

HIRS is a completely residential, co-educational, public school located outside the township of Siliguri in North Bengal. Its enormous 126-acre, tree-clad campus with its vast green playing fields nestling in a picturesque bend of the river Karthwa just below the foothills of the mighty Himalayas is an ideal location for the nurture of young bodies, minds and souls in the lap of Nature.
